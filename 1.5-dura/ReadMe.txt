++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
Menu Controls for Keyboard:
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
================================================================================================
Open:  F4 or * on the numpad
Up:    Numpad 8, Arrow Up
Down:  Numpad 2, Arrow Down
Left:  Numpad 4, Arrow Left
Right: Numpad 6, Arrow Right
Enter: Numpad 5, Enter Key
Back:  Numpad 0, Backspace
================================================================================================

++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
Menu Controls for Controller:
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
================================================================================================
Open:  Right D-Pad and RB [Xbox] or R1 [PS4 DualShock 4]
Up:    D-Pad Up
Down:  D-Pad Down
Left:  D-Pad D-Pad Left
Right: D-Pad Right
Enter: A [Xbox] or X [PS4 DualShock 4]
Back:  B [Xbox] or O [PS4 DualShock 4]
